--- title: Penne with Spinach and Ricotta tags: - recipes active_time: 20 minutes total_time: 20 minutes serves: 4 to 6 adapted_from: Educated Palate --- ### Ingredients * 1 pound spinach * 1/2 medium onion, chopped * 3 tablespoon butter * 1 pound penne * 1 cup whole milk ricotta * 3/4 cup heavy cream * 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg * 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese * salt and pepper ### Procedure 1. Cook pasta until *al dente*. 1. While pasta is cooking, sauté spinach in 1 tablespoon of butter for 5–6 minutes until tender. 1. While spinach is cooking, cook onion in remaining 2 tablespoons of butter until golden, about 5 minutes. 1. Once spinach is done, drain in a colander and squeeze out as much water is possible. Transfer to cutting board and finely chop. 1. Once onion is ready, add spinach and cook for 5 minutes, stirring often. 1. Add ricotta, cream, and nutmeg. Cook until ricotta is heated through and cream is reduced, about 2 minutes. Taste and season with salt and pepper. Remove from heat. 1. When pasta is done, drain well and toss with sauce and Parmesan. Serve immediately.
