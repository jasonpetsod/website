--- title: Oreo-Stuffed Chocolate Chip Cookies tags: - recipes active_time: 25 minutes total_time: 40 minutes yields: 2 dozen adapted_from: Picky Palate --- ### Ingredients * 1 cup butter (2 sticks), softened * 3/4 cup brown sugar * 1 cup granulated sugar * 2 large eggs * 1 tablespoon vanilla extract * 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour * 1 teaspoon salt * 1 teaspoon baking soda * 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips * Oreo cookies ### Procedure 1. Preheat oven to 350°F. 1. Cream butter and sugars together until well-combined. 1. Mix in eggs and vanilla. 1. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, salt, and baking soda. Slowly add to wet ingredients along with the chocolate chips until just combined. 1. Using a cookie scoop (or tablespoon), take one scoop (about 1.5 tbsp) of cookie dough and place it on top of an Oreo cookie. Take another scoop of dough and place on the bottom of the Oreo. Seal edges together by pressing and cupping until the Oreo is enclosed by dough. 1. Bake for 9--13 minutes. 1. Let cool 5 minutes before transferring to cooling rack.
