--- title: Brownies tags: - recipes active_time: 10 minutes total_time: 30 to 40 minutes yields: 1 to 2 dozen adapted_from: Mark Bittman, How to Cook Everything. --- ### Ingredients * 2 ounces unsweetened chocolate, roughly chopped * 8 tablespoons butter (1 stick) * 1 cup sugar * 2 eggs * 1/2 cup all-purpose flour * pinch salt * 1/2 tsp vanilla extract * 1/4 tsp mint extract * 1 cup chocolate chips ### Procedure 1. Preheat oven to 350°F. 1. Grease an 8-inch square baking pan. 1. Combine chocolate and butter over low heat, stirring occasionally. When chocolate is just about melted, remove from heat and continue to stir until smooth. 1. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir in sugar. 1. Gently stir in the flour, salt, vanilla extract, mint extract, and chocolate chips. Pour and scrape into prepared baking pan. 1. Bake for 20–25 minutes until just barely set in the middle. Cool on a rack before cutting.
