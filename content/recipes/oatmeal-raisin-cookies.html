--- title: Oatmeal Raisin Cookies tags: - recipes active_time: xx minutes total_time: xx minutes serves: 36 cookies --- ### Ingredients * 3 large eggs * 1 cup raisins * 1 tsp vanilla * 1 cup butter (two sticks) * 1 cup brown sugar * 1 cup white sugar * 2.5 cups flour * 1 tsp salt * 1 tsp cinnamon * 2 tsp baking soda * 2 cups oats * 3/4 cups nuts ### Procedure 1. Preheat oven to 375°F. 1. Cream butter and sugar. 1. Add eggs and vanilla. 1. Blend in flour, salt, cinnamon, and baking soda. Mix well. 1. Add oatmeal, raisins, nuts. 1. Bake for 11–13 minutes.
