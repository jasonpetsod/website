--- title: Beef Stew with Mushrooms tags: - recipes active_time: 30 minutes total_time: 1.5 hours serves: 8 adapted_from: The Pioneer Woman --- ### Ingredients * 2 pounds beef stew meat * 2 tablespoons flour * 4 tablespoons butter * 2 tablespoons olive oil * 2 shallots, minced * 3 cloves garlic, minced * 8 ounces cremini mushrooms * 1/2 cup red wine * 1/2 can beef consomme * salt and pepper, to taste * pasta, cooked and drained * 2 sprigs fresh thyme * 2 tablespoons flour ### Procedure 1. Sprinkle flour over meat. Toss to coat. 1. Melt butter with olive oil in heavy pot. 1. Sear meat over high heat in batches; remove to a plate when brown. Don't cook for too long or else the meat will get tough. 1. Add shallots and garlic to pan and saute for 2 minutes over medium-low heat. 1. Add mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes. 1. Pour in wine, consomme, and an extra half-can of water. Then add salt and pepper to taste, and stir. 1. Bring to a boil, then add the browned meat. Reduce heat to low. Add thyme sprigs to pot. 1. Cover and simmer for 30 to 45 minutes until meat is tender. 1. Mix 2 tablespoons flour with a little water and pour into the stew. Allow to cook and thicken for 10 more minutes. 1. Turn off heat and allow stew to sit for 15 to 20 minutes before serving.
