--- title: Guacamole tags: - recipes active_time: 10 minutes total_time: 10 minutes serves: 4 to 6 --- ### Ingredients * 2 ripe avocados, peeled and cored * 3 cloves garlic, minced * 3 serrano peppers, minced * 1/2 cup cilantro, minced * juice from 1 lime * salt to taste ### Procedure 1. Combine all ingredients and mash with a potato masher or mortar and pestle.
